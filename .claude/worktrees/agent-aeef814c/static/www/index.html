Three Ways to Work

Match it, build it, or compare it

NGW adapts to your workflow with three purpose-built modes.

Match a Look

Upload a reference photo. NGW decodes the lighting and gives you a complete recreation guide with your gear.

Build From Scratch

Choose a mood, subject type, and style. NGW generates a full lighting setup from scratch — no reference photo needed.

Shot Match

Take your shot, then compare it side-by-side with the reference. NGW evaluates the match and suggests precise adjustments.

Shoot Mode

Match any reference with your own kit

Tell NGW what lights and modifiers you own. When you analyze a reference, Shoot Mode maps the recommendation to your specific gear — with real distances, angles, and power settings. Take test shots and NGW evaluates your results.
