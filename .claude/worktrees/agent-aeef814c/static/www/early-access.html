What NGW does

Your second pair of eyes on every photo.

35+ Vision Passes
Shadow geometry, catchlight topology, highlight axis, specular classification, penumbra gradients — all from a single reference image.
Archetype Matching
Identifies the lighting style behind any photo — Hurley, Karsh, Penn, Leibovitz, and more — with confidence scores and matched signals.
Actionable Diagrams
Get a precise lighting diagram — light positions, modifiers, power ratios, distance — ready to hand to an assistant or walk into the studio with.
Shot Match
Upload your reference. Upload your attempt. NGW shows you exactly where the lighting diverges and what to adjust.
Build From Scratch
Tell NGW the look you want. It builds a full lighting plan calibrated to your gear, space, and subject — step by step.
Master Class Recipes
Guided recreations of legendary lighting setups — Caravaggio chiaroscuro, Vermeer window light, Heisler adaptive — with live coaching.
