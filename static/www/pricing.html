Frequently asked questions

Can I try Pro features before committing?
Yes. Every new account gets a 14-day free trial of Pro features. No credit card required.
What counts as an “analysis”?
Each reference photo you upload and process counts as one analysis. Re-viewing results from a previous analysis does not count.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.
What about the API?
The Studio plan includes full REST API access for integrating NGW analysis into your own tools and workflows. Rate limits apply.
