Built for photographers who take lighting seriously.

NGW combines computer vision, physics simulation, and AI to deliver actionable lighting intelligence from any reference photo.

🔍

27-Pass Vision Pipeline

Every photo is analyzed through 27 specialized computer vision passes. Shadow geometry, catchlight detection, specular surface mapping, color temperature estimation, penumbra gradients, inverse-square fall-off modeling, surface classification, and more — all from a single image.

🤖

AI-Powered Reconstruction

A vision-language model examines each photo to extract information that pure computer vision cannot detect: expression nuance, cosmetic details, styling context, and framing decisions. The VLM enriches the pipeline output without replacing any CV analysis.

🎯

Pattern Matching

NGW's reference matcher scores your analysis against a curated library of classic lighting patterns. Rembrandt, butterfly, loop, split, clamshell, broad, short, and more — each with multiple weighted dimensions for accurate classification.

📷

Shoot Mode & Kit Matching

Tell NGW what lights and modifiers you own. When you analyze a reference photo, Shoot Mode maps the recommendation to your specific gear with real distances, angles, and power settings. Take test shots and NGW evaluates your results.

📐

Auto-Generated Lighting Diagrams

Every analysis produces a top-down lighting diagram showing key, fill, rim, and background lights with their positions, angles, heights, and modifiers. Diagrams are interactive in the app and exportable as images.

📚

Reference Library

Browse a curated collection of classic lighting patterns. Each reference includes a full analysis breakdown, setup specifications, and a lighting diagram you can follow step by step.

🔧

Quick Fixes & Recommendations

After analysis, NGW suggests specific adjustments to improve your setup. Raise the key light, move a modifier closer, adjust the fill ratio — each recommendation is tied to a specific pipeline observation.
